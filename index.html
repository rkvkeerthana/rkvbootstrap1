About us

RKV College of Technology established in the year 2012 RKV Charitable and Educational Trust., located in Karaikudi, Sivagangai district ,Tamilnadu,India. The college is approved by AICTE, New Delhi, Alagappa University ,Karaikudi, Accredited by National Board of Accreditation, New Delhi and an ISO 9001:2008 Certified Institution. UG Level: ECE, Mechanical,BME,EEE,Automation and CSE are NBA (National Board of Accreditation) accredited. it is certified with NAAC with A+ Grade.

RKV CT Pride

For promoting co-curricular activities, RKV CT has an association called as RKV CT PRIDE, where PRIDE stands for Projects Research Innovation Design and Entrepreneurship. RKV CT PRIDE scouts for competitions conducted by government & private sectors on one side and on the other the student talents were analysed through various internal competitions and hackathons. Then the right talent is pitched in at the right place and through this RKV CT PRIDE Initiative RKV CT students have won many national competitions. To name a few, the Chattra Vishwakarma Award - a national level competition conducted by AICTE and Mitsubishi Electric Cup – a national level automation competition both these were won twice, RKV CT students team reached the top ten of Flipkart’s GRID competition consecutively for two years, won many awards in eYantra Competitions – an initiative of IIT Bombay & MHRD, received the top prize of one lakh in Smart India Hackathon (SIH) in the year 2022– conducted by AICTE and Ministry of Education, won the top prize of five lakhs with incubation support in India Innovation Challenge Design Contest (IICDC) - a national level competition conducted by Texas Instruments & Department of Science and Technology and many more.

RKV Center for Research and development

RKV College of Technology has established "RKV's Centre for Research & Development" (CRD), to provide high-quality research training for students, The Centre for Research & Development was established and inaugurated by the renowned scientist of ISRO and the Project Director of Chandrayaan I and Chandrayaan II, Dr.Mylswamy Annadurai on 14 April 2012. Center of Excellence(COE) The COE provides the students with access to the latest technological developments, research opportunities and industry experts. The COE also helps in connecting the students to the industry and the global trends.
